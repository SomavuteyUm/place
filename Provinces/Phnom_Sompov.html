Battambang

Phnom Sampov or it's often called Sampov Mountain is located in Phnom Sampov commune, Banan district, Battambang province and far about ~16km away from Lok Ta Dombong Khieu Nhung. Phnom Sampov is a natural resort where tourists are interested in visiting, especially local tourists on holidays or other festivals like Khmer New Year or Pchum Ben.

Note

  • Hotels: 3-star averaging KHR 98,328.

Proximity

  1. 23 min (16.1 km) by car from Krong Battambang
